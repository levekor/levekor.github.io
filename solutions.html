Industry-Specific Solutions

Medical Software, Technology & Device

Data solutions that help you immediately identify potential target market, enhancing your sales planning and marketing effectiveness, reducing the cost of prospecting, qualifying, and closing.

  • Provider Prescribing Trends: Tools for finding annual prescribing volumes of any medicine by any provider for trend analysis and sales planning
  • Patient Analytics: Tools for identifying patient populations that would be candidates to receive treatment/medical devices using CMS NCD's.
  • Cost-Reimbursement Statistics: Specialized platforms to assist in visualizing the cost of care and potential reimbursement of treatment options using current CMS data and billing codes

Government & Procurement

Custom intelligence solutions designed specifically for government contractors, procurement consultants, and public sector vendors.

  • Contract Opportunity Intelligence: Predictive analytics identifying upcoming procurement opportunities based on historical award patterns and agency spending cycles
  • Competitive Landscape Mapping: Comprehensive analysis of competitor win rates, contract values, and market positioning across federal, state, and local government segments
  • Bid Strategy Optimization: Data-driven recommendations for pricing, teaming partnerships, and proposal focus areas based on successful award patterns in specific agencies and contract types

Real Estate & Investment Services

Custom intelligence solutions designed specifically for investors, buyers, real estate firms, and asset management firms.

  • Market Opportunity Analysis: Identification of emerging investment markets through integrated analysis of property values, transaction volumes, and development permit trends
  • Investment Risk Assessment: Comprehensive property-level risk scoring combining tax assessment data, zoning changes, and neighborhood demographic shifts
  • Development Site Selection: Data-driven site recommendations based on permit approval rates, infrastructure capacity, and comparable project performance metrics

International Trade & Supply Chain

Custom intelligence solutions designed specifically for importers, exporters, and global supply chain managers.

  • Supply Chain Diversification Planning: Risk-adjusted supplier recommendations based on trade flow patterns, regulatory stability, and tariff trajectory forecasting
  • Tariff Impact Optimization: Strategic sourcing recommendations that minimize duty costs through analysis of classification changes, trade agreement benefits, and country-specific rate structures
  • Market Entry Intelligence: Country and product-specific trade opportunity identification through analysis of import demand trends, competitive positioning, and regulatory barriers
